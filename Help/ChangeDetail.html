Change Detail Level

This setting allows you to change the detail level the game is rendering while moving, and to return it to a preferred level when stopped.

This was a feature of the original CityBinder, and was much more necessary in the early 2000s, as moving quickly with detail set to high could be bad for framerates on the graphics hardware of the time.

It's included here in case it's something you want to play with, or in case you are running the game on retro / vintage hardware.

In the detail range slider control, below, the left slider controls the zoomed-out moving detail level, and the right slider controls the zoomed-in stationary detail level.

(Note that this is the opposite of the camera detail slider, where the "low" value is stationary and the "high" value is moving.)
