Custom Binds

Custom Binds come in three flavors, Simple, Complex, and Buffer.

A Simple Bind is a custom bind that can perform a string of commands. For example, a bind could be created that changes costume, performs an emote, and says the character's battle cry to the group channel.

A Complex Bind is a keybind that will perform strings of commands, in rotation, with multiple presses of a key. A Complex Bind could be created that, on first press of the bound key, will change to a given costume and change the game's windows colors to match. On a second press of the key, the bind could change to a different costume, and again change the window colors to match. After the final step, the Complex Bind will "wrap around" to the first step. A Complex Bind could be thought of as a series of Simple Binds that are performed in rotation.

A Buffer Bind is a type of keybind designed to help players buff their team members using multiple presses of the same key. A Buffer Bind can contain one or more buffing powers, with an optional chat/emote step to be activated with each power. The Buffer Bind pane allows you to specify a bind key for each team member and for each pet. The first press of a given key will select the associated team member or pet. Subsequent presses of the key will activate the buffing powers, in order. This allows you to buff each of your team members and/or pets with quick repetitive presses of one key per target.
