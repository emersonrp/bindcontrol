Fly Key Action

The Fly Key Action picker determines what the Fly Key will do when pressed. Some options might not be available depending on your power picks and/or Speed on Demand settings.

  • Speed on Demand - the Fly Key will toggle between Fly SoD Mode and your Default SoD Mode as configured in the Speed on Demand Settings box.
  • Power Toggle - the Fly Key will toggle between your Primary and Defensive Fly powers. If either is unavailable, this will toggle the other one on and off. If Speed on Demand is active with another powerset, it will be turned off until it is reset with Speed on Demand Toggle or Reset Keybinds.
  • None - the Fly Key will be disabled.
