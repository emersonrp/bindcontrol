BindControl's Output Files

This is a high-level overview of the various directories and files output by BindControl when you click the "Write Binds" button. These will be written to the profile-specific binds directory configured within BindControl.

reset.txt
This is the bindfile that gets loaded when you press your "Reset Keybinds" key. It reloads and resets all of BindControl's state to be as if you had just installed the binds for the first time. Simple Binds from the Custom Binds page and some of the binds from the General and Gameplay pages end up in here, as well as the initial/base state of most binds from the Movement Power page.
cbinds buff wiz
These directories are created from the Custom Binds page. Complex Binds will all drop their bindfiles in the cbinds directory, Buffer Binds will create bindfiles in the buff directory, and various Bind Wizard binds will drop their bindfiles in the wiz directory.
teamsel teamsel2
These directories are generated by the Gameplay page, and contain the bindfiles for the two different team selection keybind schemes.
nova.txt dwarf.txt
These files are created by the Movement Powers page when the Profile is for a Peacebringer or Warshade and any Kheldian-specific keys are configured.
R F J S N Q AR AF AJ AS AN AQ FR FF FJ FS FN FQ BO GBO
These one- and two-letter directories are created from the Speed On Demand binds, depending on how the Movement Powers page is configured. No configuration will have all of these directories, but a typical Speed on Demand setup could have as many as eight or ten of them.
dtp tp ttp
These directories are also created by the Movement Powers page, and pertain to the various teleport power binds. dtp contains files for Kheldian Dwarf teleport powers, tp standard teleport powers, and ttp Team Teleport.
mmb petsel
These directories are created by the Mastermind page. mmb contains the various bindfiles needed for the Pet Action Binds, while petsel contains the bindfiles for the rotational pet selection keybinds.
