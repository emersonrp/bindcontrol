Turn to Match Camera

This checkbox determines what happens when the player moves forward while the camera is facing a different direction. If checked, the player will change direction to match the current camera facing. If not, the player will move forward relative to the player's facing, which will be at an angle relative to the camera.

Turning to face the camera angle is the default with Homecoming's "Modern" keybind scheme. Previous to that, the default was to move relative to the player's facing.
