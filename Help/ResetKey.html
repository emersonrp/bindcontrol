Reset Key

The Binds Reset Key will reload all of your keybinds and reset any "stateful" ones to their default state.

Depending on how you have the "Set binds to default on reset" Preferences checkbox, this will optionally restore the game's original keybinds, then apply all BindControl-configured keybinds.

If you have Speed on Demand binds set up, it will stop all movement and reset your Speed on Demand Mode to the default.

Any Complex or Buffer Binds will be reset to start at the beginning of their rotation on the next keypress.
