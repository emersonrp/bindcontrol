Speed Power Key Action

The Speed Power Key Action picker determines what the Speed Power Key will do when pressed. Some options might not be available depending on your power picks and/or Speed on Demand settings.

  • Speed on Demand - the Speed Power Key will toggle between Super Speed SoD Mode and your default SoD Mode as configured in the Speed on Demand Settings box.
  • Main Power Toggle - the Speed Power Key will simply toggle your primary speed power on and off.
  • None - the Speed Power Key will be disabled.
