Speed Key Action

The Speed Key Action picker determines what the Speed Key will do when pressed. Some options might not be available depending on your power picks and/or Speed on Demand settings.

  • Speed on Demand - the Speed Key will toggle between Super Speed SoD Mode and your Default SoD Mode as configured in the Speed on Demand Settings box.
  • Power Toggle - the Speed Key will toggle your primary speed power on and off. If Speed on Demand is active with another powerset, it will be turned off until it is reset with Speed on Demand Toggle or Reset Keybinds.
  • None - the Speed Key will be disabled.
