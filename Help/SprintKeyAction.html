Sprint Power Key Action

The Sprint Power Key Action picker determines what the Sprint Power Key will do when pressed. Some options might not be available depending on your power picks and/or Speed on Demand settings.

  • Speed on Demand - the Sprint Power Key will toggle between Sprint SoD Mode and your Default SoD Mode as configured in the Speed on Demand Settings box.
  • Power Toggle - the Sprint Power Key will toggle your sprint power on and off. If Speed on Demand is active with another powerset, it will be turned off until it is reset with Speed on Demand Toggle or Reset Keybinds.
  • None - the Sprint Power Key will be disabled.
