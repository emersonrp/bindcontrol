qwy Numpad Binds

The qwy binds are a Mastermind keybind scheme posted to the Homecoming forums by user qwy in 2020.

They use modifier keys and the number pad to select pets, individually or in groups, and to give them orders.

They also optionally use a custom window and a popmenu to access commands. Use the checkboxes to select whether to install either or both of these. Both are recommended, but require that you have your "Game Dir" set up correctly in the Preferences dialog.
